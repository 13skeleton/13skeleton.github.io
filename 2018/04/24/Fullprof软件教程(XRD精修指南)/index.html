XRD 精修指南

Fullprof软件教程之XRD 精修

精修顺序

A sensible sequence of refinement of a crystal structure is the following:

  1. Scale factor.
  2. Scale factor, zero point of detector , 1rst background parameter and lattice constants. In case of very sloppy background, it may be wise to actually refine at least two background parameters, or better fix the background using linear interpolation between a set of fixed points provided by user.
  3. Add the refinement of atomic positions and (eventually) an overall Debye-Waller factor, especially for high temperature data.
  4. Add the peak shape and asymmetry parameters.
  5. Add atom occupancies (if required).
  6. Turn the overall temperature factor into individual isotropic thermal parameters.
  7. Include additional background parameters (if background is refined).
  8. Refine the individual anisotropic thermal parameters if the quality of the data is good enough.
  9. In case of constant wavelength data, the parameters Sycos and/or Sysin to correct for instrumental or physical aberrations with a COS or SIN angular dependence. 2θ
  10. Microstructural parameters: size and strain effects.

精修准备文件

  1. 晶体结构文件(.cif)*

通过icsd或其他数据库找到对应XRD的晶体结构文件

  1. XRD衍射数据文件(.dat)*
  • 测试要求:

**通常XRD数据要求采用步进模式测得,且 $2\theta$ 在$10^{\circ} - 120^{\circ}$度范围内，步长通常为$0.005^{\circ}$，$0.01^{\circ}$，$0.02^{\circ}$ **

  • 数据格式

打开FullProf软件，选择WinPlotr插件，选择open pattern file，选择INSTRM

=0:free F.(Ti,step,Tf)，导入*.dat文件

其具体格式要求如下:

  1. 第一行可以用"#"或者"!"表示注释。

For all the following formats, WinPLOTR read but doesn't take into account the first lines with "#" or "!" as first character. This can help the user to transform your data files compatible with one of the following WinPLOTR format.

  1. 文件第一行: 起始角度 步长 终止角度，文件后续行: 强度值

INSTRM=0: free format file (extension: .dat)`

  • line 1 : 2Theta_min(deg.) step(deg.) 2Theta_max(deg.) + comments
  • lines l : counting
  • rk: sigmas(n) = SQRT(counting(n)) `
  1. 精修控制文件(.pcr)*

通常生成的pcr文件如下图:

注:

  • EPS和弛豫因子

  1. 推荐EPS=0.1

  2. R_at: 原子参数，包括原子坐标等

  3. R_an: 各向异性参数

  4. R_pr: 峰形参数，晶格常数，择优取向等

  5. R_gl: 全局参数，比如零点漂移，背底等

  • 背底模型

    在第i步中背底强度bi通常可以通过以下三种方法获得

  1. 特定的背底函数，通常为多项式polynomial

  2. 在XRD图谱中手动选点，然后采用线性插值的方法

  3. 用户提供的特定函数获得背底强度 背底的控制

Nba: =0 通过多项式来精修背底 = 1 从COFIL.bac文件中读取背底 =2，3 在给定的N个点中采用线性插值获得背底

  • 峰形函数的选择Npr

Npr=0 Gaussian(G) 高斯函数 Npr=1 Lorentzian(L) 洛伦兹函数 Npr=5 pseudo-Voigt函数 通常选择Npr=5，此过程主要精修的参数为$H_{K}$, $\eta_{0}(shape)$,X

以下为主要公式:

$$\frac{C_{0}^{0.5}}{H_{K} \pi^{0.5}} \exp \left(-C_{0} (2 \theta_{1}-2 \theta_{k})^{2} /H_{k}^{2} \right) $$

$$\frac{C_{1}^{0.5}}{\pi H_{K}} \times \frac{1}{\left[1+C_{1} \frac{(2\theta_{i}-2\theta_{k})^{2}}{H_{K}^{2}}\right]}$$

$$\eta L +(1-\eta)G$$

$$\eta=\eta_{0}+X*(2\theta)$$
